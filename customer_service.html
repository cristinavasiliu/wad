Back in 2005, the internet was already wildly popular, but online shopping hadn't quite taken hold yet. Their plans to sell thousands of dollars worth of instruments, equipment and accessories online were met with laughter. Luckily they understood what their potential customers were looking for; one smart supplier understood that too and agreed to go into business with them. After that, the duo set to work putting a webshop online and renting a 500 ftÂ² storage space. In no time at all, they were shipping products far and wide, and Gamma Store was born.

As musicians, we know that checking out gear at a physical store is an experience in and of itself, but we also know that not everyone wants to do that for every little thing they need. That's why it's important to us to be able to provide you with the best possible online store experience possible. That means we provide extensive product information, high-resolution photos and audio snipets so you can always find the perfect product.

Delivery & Returns

When you place your order, you can choose between standard delivery via ParcelForce, UPS Standard, or pay extra for UPS Express delivery. As soon as your order has been packed and prepared, we'll send you an email including the Track & Trace information for your package. If your Track & Trace code isn't active yet, it just means the package is waiting to be collected and scanned in by the courier. Please wait a little while and check again later.If you miss the courier, a note will be left letting you know when they'll try again or that the package has been left with your neighbour.

30 Day Satisfaction Guarantee - Limitations and Exceptions Apply*

If you're not satisfied, neither are we. If for any reason you're not completely satisfied with your purchase, simply return it in its original condition within the return period and we'll give you a full refund of the purchase price, excluding shipping and handling charges. Your refund will be promptly processed when we have confirmed that your returned item meets these requirements.
